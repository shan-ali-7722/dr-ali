About Me

Passionate Physical Therapist

I am a passionate physical therapy graduate with extensive hands-on experience in neurological and orthopedic rehabilitation and therapeutic modalities. My expertise lies in developing individualized treatment plans and employing advanced manual therapy techniques to enhance patient outcomes.

With a Doctor of Physical Therapy degree from Government College University Faisalabad and ongoing MPhil studies at King Edward Medical University, I combine clinical practice with academic excellence to provide evidence-based care.

My approach to physiotherapy integrates the latest research with personalized care, ensuring that each patient receives the most effective treatment for their specific condition. I am committed to continuous professional development, regularly publishing research and attending conferences to stay at the forefront of physical therapy practices.

Areas of Expertise

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Specialized post-operative rehabilitation for ACL, TKR (Total Knee Replacement), and THR (Total Hip Replacement) surgeries, ensuring optimal recovery and return to function.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Comprehensive treatment approaches for patients with neurological conditions, focusing on restoring mobility, function, and quality of life.

Manual Therapy Techniques

Advanced hands-on techniques including Kaltenborn methods, Mulligan's Mobilization with Movement (MWM), and Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation (PNF).

Therapeutic Exercise

Customized exercise protocols designed to improve strength, flexibility, endurance, and overall function for various musculoskeletal conditions.

Muscle Energy Techniques (MET)

Specialized approach using isometric contractions to relax and lengthen muscles, particularly effective for treating lumbosacral dysfunction.

Intensive Care Rehabilitation

Early intervention and rehabilitation for critically ill patients in ICU settings, promoting faster recovery and preventing complications.
